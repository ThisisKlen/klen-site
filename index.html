03 — MARKET PROBLEM

The protein snack category is broken.
Consumers deserve better.

🚫

Tastes like medicine

Most protein bars are chalky, dry, and artificial. Consumers eat them out of obligation — not enjoyment. The experience punishes the choice rather than rewarding it.

The category is dominated by hardcore fitness aesthetics. Aggressive branding leaves out the 80% of consumers who simply want a smarter daily snack.

🏷️

Misleading nutrition claims

Hidden sugars and inflated protein numbers erode trust. Consumers are sophisticated — and they notice when brands aren't being honest.
